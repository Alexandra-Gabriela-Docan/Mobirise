About Me

Greetings, I'm Sophia, and I warmly welcome you to my creative haven – a portfolio that embodies my lifelong passion for interior design.

Design, for me, is not just about aesthetics; it's a means to weave narratives, evoke emotions, and create spaces that resonate with individuality. With an unwavering love for beauty and an innate desire to make the ordinary extraordinary, I've dedicated my life to crafting spaces that inspire, comfort, and captivate.

Each design within these pages reflects a unique chapter of my journey – from humble beginnings to the culmination of years of dedication. From the delicate balance of colors to the harmonious fusion of textures, every project I undertake is a canvas on which I paint the dreams and desires of my clients.

Educational Background


1. Bachelor's Degree in Interior Design: This comprehensive program exposed her to fundamental design principles, spatial planning, color theory, and the use of various design software tools.
2. Master's Degree in Interior Design and BIM Architecture : Building upon her foundation, Sophia later completed a Master's Degree in Environmental Design, focusing on sustainable and eco-friendly design solutions. 

Certifications and Additional Training

1. Certified Interior Designer (CID): This certification established her as a qualified interior design professional.
2. Advanced CAD and 3D Modeling Training: This expertise allows her to create detailed, realistic visualizations of her design concepts.
3. Continuing Education in Trends and Materials: She regularly attends workshops, seminars, and conferences to keep abreast of the latest design trends and innovations in materials and technology.
